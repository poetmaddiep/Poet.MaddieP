Maddie P

Poet | Speaker | Advocate

Madison “Maddie P” is a poet from Houston, Texas whose work explores identity, mental health, love, and social justice. As the 2020 Houston Youth Poet Laureate and a 2023 TEDxUTAustin speaker, Maddie’s voice has resonated across platforms, including The New York Times, where her piece “To Be a Black Girl” was featured.
